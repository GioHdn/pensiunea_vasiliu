Program

We invite you to discover the riches of the Delta through programs between Land and Sea.
The idea is to offer you a complete overview of the Delta, both from a fauna and flora point of view but also from a cultural point of view with, for example, a visit to the city of Sulina.

Descriptive

Day 1 : Transfer to Crisan by boat from the port of Tulcea (center or downstream port), accommodation and dinner at the pension. Free walk in the village...
Day 2 and 3: Day trip to Mila 23, Caraorman or Letea. Picnic by the water, dinner and accommodation at the pension
Day 3 : Day dedicated to the practice of Canoeing Very quiet itinerary, accessible to all. Dinner and accommodation at the pension
Day 4 : Day trip to the city of Sulina. On the program, discovery of the city and idleness on the beach. You can choose between return to Tulcea, night in Sulina or night in Crisan...

Our strengths

- Varied program: birdwatching, canoeing and beach
- Pickup from Tulcea or Bucharest
- Accessible to all
- Flexible program
- Possibility to extend your stay by one or two nights
