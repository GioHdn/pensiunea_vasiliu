Canoe

Whether you are a beginner or experienced in the practice of this hobby, whether you want to rent a canoe for one or more days, we have several routes around Crisan that will allow you to discover the beauties of our environment in peace. Our canoes are stable and easy to handle.

Characteristics

- Double canoe (for 2 people)
- Life jacket and waterproof bag provided
- For all audiences

Possible routes

Excursion accessible to all : you set off on a small canal behind the pension to visit Lake Jacob, a large lake where the fishermen of the village set their nets. It is also a privileged place to observe Pelicans and other species. Very quiet route suitable for rowing sports.

Excursion for advanced audiences : Crisan-Caraorman-Crisan, Crisan-Mila 23-Crisan, Crisan-Letea-Crisan. These are multi-day itineraries. Contact us for more details.

Rates

35 euros/day/canoe
