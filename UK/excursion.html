Excursions

Due to its geographical location, CRISAN is the ideal place to discover the Danube Delta with an eco-friendly approach. The environment is extremely varied: large lakes, tangles of small canals, reed beds, primary forests...
We offer day trips from Crisan, the duration of the outing is 6 hours minimum. You will be accompanied by one of the best guides in the Delta.

Day trip from Crisan

• Excursion to the north and the village of Mila 23.
We cross the old branch of the Danube, narrow canals lined with willows, numerous lakes as well as large expanses of white and yellow water lilies. Total immersion in the wild world of the Delta to meet pelicans, kingfishers, grebes...

• Excursion to the South and Caraorman.
Extraordinarily rich, this part of the Delta offers you a majestic spectacle with pelicans, ibises, rollers, night herons, as well as beautiful landscapes with a very relaxing atmosphere...

• Excursion to the village of Letea and its primary forest.
After a morning on the water and beautiful wild encounters, we arrive in the village of Letea. A cart is waiting for us to discover the village of Letea and the forest. The environment is unique, between old oaks, sand dunes and wild horses.
